Depending on the extent of the provider’s practice, patient portals hold everything from yearly healthcare expenditures to eyeglasses prescriptions and lab results. No more shuffling papers to find receipts or records after a visit!

Are patient portals safe?

Of patient portal benefits and challenges, however, probably the biggest concern is the security of a patient’s sensitive medical data. Your office cannot simply set up a free website and expect patients to allow their medical data to be posted there! But good news: patient portals are safe. Governed by the rules of HIPAA regarding sensitive health information, these portals are designed for security and hosted on a secure site. Choosing a webhost that offers a secure, HIPPA-compliant service with frequent upgrades, responsive speeds, and limited downtime can go a long way towards encouraging patients to use your patient portal.
